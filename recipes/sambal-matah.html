Sambal Matah Recipe

Description

Sambal matah is a vibrant Indonesian raw chili relish made from finely chopped shallots, lemongrass, and chilies, all mixed with a splash of lime juice and coconut oil. It delivers a fresh, tangy, and spicy flavor, often used as a zesty accompaniment to grilled meats and seafood.

Ingredients

  • Shallots
  • Bird's eye chilies
  • Lemongrass
  • Galangal
  • Kaffir lime leaves
  • Lime Juice
  • Coconut Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Finely Finely slice shallots, bird's eye chilies, lemongrass, and, if using, galangal and kaffir lime leaves.
  2. Mix Ingredients: In a bowl, combine the sliced shallots, chilies, lemongrass, and optional galangal and kaffir lime leaves.
  3. Add Seasonings: Pour in lime juice and coconut oil. Stir well to mix all ingredients thoroughly.
  4. Adjust Flavor: Season with salt and a touch of sugar, if desired, to balance the flavors. Mix again.
  5. Rest and Serve: Let the sambal sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Serve as a condiment with your favorite grilled meats, seafood, or other dishes.
