Easy Stovetop Rice Recipe

Description

Simple and easy stovetop cooked rice to bring together Balinesian Pork Belly and Sambal Matah

Ingredients

  • Long Grain Rice, such as Jasmine Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Rinse and Combine:
    1. Rinse 1 cup of rice under cold water until the water runs clear. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed rice with 2 cups of water.
  2. Cook:
    1. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid, and simmer—15-20 minutes for white rice, 40-45 minutes for brown rice—without lifting the lid.
  3. Rest and Fluff:
    1. Remove the pot from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5-10 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
